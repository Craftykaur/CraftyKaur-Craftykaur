How a piece is made

Thread to doorstep

Four steps, same order, every time — from your first message to the ribbon on the box.

i

Tell me the story

Names, the date, the colours of the day — sent over DM or the form below.

ii

I sketch the hoop

A hand-drawn layout so you approve the pose, palette and calendar before a single stitch.

iii

Stitch, drape, or cast

Embroidery is hooped and hand-sewn; resin pieces are pressed, poured, and cured in layers.

iv

Wrapped & sent

Finished, photographed for you, and packed with tissue and a bow before it travels.
